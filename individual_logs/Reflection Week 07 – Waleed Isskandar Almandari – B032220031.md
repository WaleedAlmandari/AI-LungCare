1. How does effective communication reduce project risks?

Effective communication reduces project risks by making sure that all stakeholders clearly understand the project goals, scope, and responsibilities. When updates and issues are shared early, potential problems such as misunderstandings, delays, or incorrect assumptions can be addressed before they become serious risks. This also helps ensure that decisions are made on time and based on accurate information.
